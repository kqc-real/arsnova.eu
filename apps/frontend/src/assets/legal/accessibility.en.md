# Accessibility

**Last updated: July 20, 2026**

arsnova.eu is designed so that you can use the application with a keyboard, a screen reader, high levels of zoom, and your operating system’s contrast settings. This page explains the accessibility support available to you and how we maintain it.

## Our standard

We follow the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/) at conformance level AA.

The application has undergone both technical and manual accessibility testing, including tests with keyboards, screen readers, high levels of zoom, and system contrast modes. Automated checks are also part of our development process. We repeat and extend these tests whenever the application changes.

## Navigation and operation

You can use all essential functions with a keyboard. A skip link takes you directly to the main content, visible focus indicators show your current position, and a clear heading structure makes the application easier to navigate.

Screen readers receive concise status messages when important content changes, for example when you join a session, submit an answer, or move to a new quiz phase.

## Personal time in live sessions

When a question has a countdown, you can choose one of three options under **Personal time**:

- **Standard:** the session’s shared countdown
- **10× time:** ten times the personal response time
- **No deadline:** no personal countdown during the current question

Your choice is saved for the session and restored if you rejoin.

The host coordinates the shared flow of the live session. When the host releases the results, the question ends for all participants. Before changing phases, the host can see whether participants are still using personal time. After the shared countdown has ended, an open 10× time window can only be closed following explicit confirmation. This combines individual timing needs with a reliable shared session flow.

## Display and motion

For scored questions, you can hide the live points display if the changing number is distracting. Your preference remains saved on your device.

If your system uses the **Reduce motion** setting, arsnova.eu reduces decorative animations. The interface also adapts to your selected language and to light, dark, and high-contrast displays.

A structured PDF/UA profile is available for result reports, including a document title, document language, tags, and embedded fonts.

## Scope

This information applies to the features of arsnova.eu and to documents generated by the application. Accessibility information for linked third-party services is provided by their respective operators.
